ABOUT THE PROJECT

It is in the middle of the Atlantic that a new highly exclusive haven of natural and architectural beauty will rise. The ‘SAUDIA’ Madeira project will be a residential area of excellence, with large green and leisure spaces, wonderful views to the sea, which will provide a unique sunset to its residents. The 15 buildings that compose it have a modern design and all the conditions to give you a dream lifestyle.
